# Topic Detection in Customer Reviews: A Small NLP Experiment

## Context

As part of my exploration of Natural Language Processing (NLP), I experimented with a simple topic detection approach using customer reviews from a taquería.

The goal was not only to explore the technical side, but also to understand how text analysis can generate insights useful for business decisions.

## Objective

Identify recurring themes in customer comments and explore what those themes might mean from a business perspective.

## Approach

The experiment used basic NLP techniques such as:

- Text preprocessing
- Tokenization
- Stopword removal
- Frequency analysis

Libraries used:
- NLTK
- Python

## Example insights

| Word | Possible Meaning | Business Hypothesis |
|-----|-----|-----|
| queso | memorable ingredient | cheese may be a differentiating attribute |
| servicio | customer service | service quality strongly impacts perception |
| faltar | missing items | potential issues in order fulfillment |

## Business reflection

One interesting takeaway from this small experiment is that text analysis can reveal patterns that businesses might not notice immediately.

Even simple NLP techniques can help transform qualitative customer feedback into actionable insights.

## Conclusion

This experiment reinforced an idea I find fascinating:

AI and NLP are not only powerful technically — they can also help translate large volumes of customer feedback into meaningful signals for decision-making.
